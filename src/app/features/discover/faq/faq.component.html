FAQs

Frequently asked questions ordered by popularity. Remember that if the visitor has not committed to the call to action, they may still have questions (doubts) that can be answered.

How do I use Sauna Spots?

To use Sauna Spots, simply search for sauna spots in your area, view the details of each spot, and save your favorites. You can also sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on your favorite spots.

What information is available?

Sauna Spots provides information about each spot, including its location, facilities, reviews, and contact details. You can also see if a spot has been verified by the owner.

How are spots verified?

Spot owners have access to their own panel where they can add and edit information about their spot. Verified spots have been reviewed and approved by the owner.

Can I add spots?

Currently, only spot owners can add and manage spots on Sauna Spots. However, you can suggest a spot to the owner or contact us if you have a recommendation.
